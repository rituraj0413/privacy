EduSeats ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number (for OTP login and account verification)
  • Username & Password (if you choose password login)
  • Name and Library Name (during registration)
  • Device ID (generated and stored securely on your device)

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and secure login
  • To provide access to the EduSeats dashboard and services
  • To send one-time passwords (OTPs) via SMS for secure login

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (encrypted database). Device tokens are stored locally using secure storage.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. We only use Twilio or similar SMS providers to send OTPs.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and secure practices. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.
